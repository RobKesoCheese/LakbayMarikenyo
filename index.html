ABOUT DAZZLING CITY OF MARIKINA

Marikina City is proudly known as the Shoe Capital of the Philippines, a title earned through its long history of expert craftsmanship and innovation in shoemaking. Home to the country’s largest shoe industry, Marikina produces world-class footwear that blends tradition with modern design. Beyond commerce, the city celebrates this heritage with festivals, museums, and landmarks that showcase its enduring pride as the heart of Philippine shoemaking.
